## ⚙️ Weight Reduction Engineering: Magnesium Casting Mastery

Magnesium alloy high-pressure die casting (HPDC) is the frontier of lightweight industrial design. Being 33% lighter than aluminum and 75% lighter than steel, magnesium structural casting is indispensable for [electric vehicles (EV)](https://alumcasting.com/ev-battery-housing-die-casting/), defense electronics, and aerospace housings. 

[Compare our magnesium vs aluminum die casting](https://alumcasting.com/magnesium-vs-aluminum-die-casting/) to see why many clients choose conversion [weight reduction](https://alumcasting.com/aluminum-to-magnesium-conversion-weight-reduction/).

![Magnesium alloy die casting production line](/img/magnesium-die-casting-banner.webp)

### 🔬 Premium Magnesium Grades
We engineer parts using specialized magnesium alloy formulations:
* **[AZ91D](https://alumcasting.com/az91d-magnesium-die-casting-automotive-parts/):** High strength and superior anti-corrosion for electronic enclosures.
* **[AM60B / AM50A](https://alumcasting.com/am60b-magnesium-alloy-die-casting-suppliers/):** Extreme ductility and impact resistance for automotive safety components.
