Most launch delays in die casting programs are not caused by one major failure. They come from unmanaged handoffs between RFQ, DFM, tooling, trial loops, and quality validation.

This playbook gives procurement and engineering teams a practical path to shorten cycle time without increasing quality risk.

![Die casting program timeline and launch tracking](/img/automated-die-casting-production-line.webp "Lead time improves with milestone discipline")

## Typical RFQ to SOP Timeline Blocks

- RFQ and technical clarification.
- DFM review and design freeze.
- Tool design, machining, and assembly.
- T1/T2 trials with parameter optimization.
- Dimensional and functional validation.
- PPAP closure and SOP release.

## 1. RFQ Stage: Lock Inputs Early

Late clarification is one of the biggest schedule killers. Ensure the RFQ contains clear geometry, critical tolerances, quality targets, and annual demand assumptions.

Use this checklist article: [Die Casting Quote Template: RFQ Data Pack]({{< relref "blog/die-casting-quote-template-rfq-data-pack.md" >}}).

## 2. DFM Stage: Reduce Rework Before Tool Build

Freeze these items before tool steel is cut:

- Gate/runner/overflow concept.
- Parting line and ejection strategy.
- Machining datum path and stock allowance.
- Porosity-risk regions and validation plan.

Early DFM discipline removes weeks of avoidable rework later.

## 3. Tooling and Trial Stage: Plan for Fast Closure

Avoid generic "T1 in X weeks" commitments. Request:

- Detailed tool build milestones.
- Trial objective by round (fill, dimension, leak, cosmetic).
- Defined correction window and decision criteria.

For tooling scope control, see: [Tooling Cost Breakdown in Aluminum Die Casting]({{< relref "blog/tooling-cost-breakdown-aluminum-die-casting.md" >}}).

## 4. Validation Stage: Align Quality Gates With SOP

Do not wait until late stage to define acceptance:

- CMM and X-ray sampling logic.
- Leak test method and threshold.
- Capability target for key characteristics.

Reference: [Die casting factory CMM and X-ray inspection](https://alumcasting.com/die-casting-factory-cmm-xray-inspection/).

## 5. Sourcing Tactics to Shorten Lead Time

- Choose suppliers with in-house casting + CNC + validation.
- Run weekly technical reviews with decision owners present.
- Track open risks in a shared launch dashboard.
- Tie commercial milestones to measurable technical outcomes.
